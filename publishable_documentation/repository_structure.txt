Repository Structure
/publishable_data: The 4 final, compressed, and encoded Stata master datasets (Phase 4).

/cleaningCode: The sequential Python/Stata extraction and harmonization pipeline.
    /cms: Part B / Part D claim aggregations, PECOS linkage, and RVU construction.
    /hcahps: Hospital/HOPD/ASC facility quality metrics and survey harmonization.
    /mips: QPP scoring integrations and ZIP-level market imputation.
    /final_cleaning: Terminal crosswalks, merges, and optimization scripts.

/crosswalks: Highly valuable public-good mappings (RBCS taxonomies, Empirical Cost Thresholds, USAN classes).

/docs: Detailed methodology, data dictionary (Master_Codebook.md), and project specifications.
